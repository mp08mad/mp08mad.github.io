About Taskro

Taskro is a fast and elegant to-do list app designed for clarity and simplicity. Create tasks locally on your device, organize categories with custom icons, receive helpful reminders, and enjoy a clean widget for quick daily overviews.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where are my tasks stored?
All tasks are stored locally on your device to ensure privacy and speed.

2. Do I need an account?
No account is required. Taskro works instantly, without sign-in.

3. Why am I not receiving notifications?
Make sure Taskro has notification permissions enabled in Settings.

4. How do I customize categories?
Add or edit categories and choose from a large selection of icons.
