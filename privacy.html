Taskro – Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Add Date]

Taskro (“the App”) is developed by Madalin Patrinoiu (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. Information We Collect

Taskro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account creation and all data stays on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

The App stores tasks, categories, icons, notification settings, and preferences locally on your device. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

3. Push Notifications

If enabled, notifications are delivered using Apple’s iOS system. We do not collect or store any information about your notifications.

4. Widgets

Widgets display your task information directly from your device and do not send data externally.

5. Analytics and Tracking

Taskro does not use analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or any tracking technologies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. No data leaves your device.

7. Your Choices

You can delete tasks anytime or remove the App to erase all stored data. Notifications can be disabled in your device settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

Taskro does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is safe for all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available at this link.
